Hello! My name is Natasha-Kimberly Lopez, and I am currently a second-year Global Studies major enrolled at the University of California Riverside. However, I am looking to switch to Art History with Administrative Studies.

I was born in the Philippines and raised in the Bay Area, specifically in San Bruno, California. I briefly attended Capuchino High School and later transferred to Burlingame High School in Burlingame, California, where I graduated in May 2019. As I attended Burlingame High School, I also voluntarily tutored several elementary school kids from the local schools on the weekends. During my time tutoring, I learned that patience is crucial to ensuring students feel supported and motivated throughout the learning process. As I tutored, I also felt as though I became friends with the students and their families. As a result, I still keep in touch with them even after leaving for college.

My first quarter at the University of California Riverside was a refreshing, yet challenging experience due to a sense of newfound freedom. The newfound freedom meant that self-discipline was necessary to balancing academic work, a social life, and a normal sleep schedule. In order to maintain a balance between school and social activities, I utilized a planner and recorded current assignments as well as upcoming assignment deadlines and exams. In regards to my work ethic, I strongly believe in attention to detail and that in order to do so, it's necessary to create a plan or schedule ahead of time.
